Measurement of Steam Quality

VARIABLES

Outlet temperature (T2) (°C)




Outlet pressure (P2) (MPa)



CONTROLS
Variables Variables

P1 = Inlet/initial pressure
P2 = Outlet/final pressure
T2 = Outlet/final temperature
h1 = specific enthalpy of saturated state ( at inlet )
h2 = specific enthalpy of superheated state ( at outlet )
x1 = Quality of steam at inlet
hf = Specific enthalpy of saturated liquid state at pressure P1 (kJ/kg)
hg = Specific enthalpy of saturated vapor state at pressure P1 (kJ/kg)
hfg = Change in specific enthalpy during phase transition (kJ/kg)
 Formulae Formulae

h1 = h2
hfg = hg-hf
x = (h2-hf)/hfg
